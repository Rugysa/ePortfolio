Community involvement and volunteering

AC7PT

I joined this association and I'm became Vice-President because it is important to me that all students entering engineering school can find their place and continue their studies without fear of being judged.

I contributed to their outreach by writing the instragram post on TDOR (Trans day of Rememberance) and the one about the "Fete du travail" .

It was important to me because trans people often tend to be invisible and this little known event still shows all the discrimination and violence that trans people suffer.
